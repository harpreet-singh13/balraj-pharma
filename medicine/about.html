About Us

Welcome to BALRAJ PHARMACEUTICALS PVT LTD

BALRAJ Pharmaceutical Company, established in 2024 and based in Ghaziabad, is dedicated to advancing women’s health through a premium range of gynecology products. Founded with a vision to improve the quality of life for women, BALRAJ Pharma has rapidly become a trusted name in the gynecology segment. Our mission is to deliver high-quality, affordable healthcare solutions that cater to the unique needs of women, ensuring their well-being with innovative and effective products.

From our inception, we have focused on creating and offering a diverse portfolio of gynecological medicines. Among our flagship brands are Dydro 6 , known for its effectiveness in hormone regulation; Strong Boon, which supports reproductive health; Doctor NCR , available in both 10mg and 15mg, designed to address a range of gynecological conditions; ClearV3 , renowned for its clarity and efficacy in menstrual health; and OCP 21, a comprehensive solution for overall women’s health. Each of these products reflects our commitment to excellence and innovation.

Our dedication to superior quality is demonstrated through our partnerships with leading manufacturers and adherence to international quality standards, including GMP, WHO, and ISO certifications. As we continue to expand our reach, BALRAJ Pharmaceutical is building a robust network of healthcare professionals and distribution channels, ensuring that our products are both innovative and accessible. With a focus on ethical practices and a strong presence in the industry, BALRAJ Pharma is poised to make a significant impact in women’s healthcare across the country.
